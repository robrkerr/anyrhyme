What is this? ▼ ▲
anyRhyme is a rhyming dictionary that allows you to customize exactly the type of rhyme that you want. In addition to basic rhyming, you can look for assonance, consonance, alliteration, portmanteaus (e.g. sheeple), or explore words with almost any arbitrary phonetic pattern.
This webapp is powered by the anyWhere, anyRhyme API, which you can use to make cool things, such as a silly misquote generator. It uses word phoneme data from Carnegie Mellon University and lexemes (word definitions) from Princeton University's WordNet.
